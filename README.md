ğŸ“Š Key Insights
Revenue & Sales

Clothing is the top-performing category in both revenue and units sold.

Accessories follow, while footwear and outerwear underperform.

Customer Demographics

Male customers generate more total revenue.

Young Adults and Middle-aged customers contribute the majority of revenue.

Discount Behavior

Discounts are used by both low and high spenders.

Certain products rely heavily on discounting, indicating price sensitivity.

Customer Loyalty

The majority of customers fall into the Loyal segment.

Repeat buyers are significantly more likely to be subscribers.

Subscriptions

Subscribers:

Do not spend more per order

Are more likely to return and repurchase

Subscriptions improve retention, not average order value.

Shipping Behavior

Customers choosing Express shipping spend slightly more on average.

Convenience plays a role in purchase value.
